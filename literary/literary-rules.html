STORY TELLING

“ We’re all stories in the end. Make it a good one.”
Stories are like little windows into another undiscovered world. For once, you have full autonomy of how things end! Whether inspired by reality or spun with the threads of imagination, the choice is yours. 

Judging Criteria

● Voice and tone - 15
● Body language and stage presence - 10
● Character and scene composition - 15
● Audience engagement and impact of performance- 10 

Rules

● Individual participation
● Time limit - 3-6 minute
● Languages permitted - English, Tamil and hindi.
● Use of props is permitted unless they could damage the stage or are dangerous.
● Vulgarity and obscenity will not be tolerated.
● All participants must possess their college id cards.
● Participant should reach the venue 60 minutes prior to the event.

British parliamentary debate

Have you ever imagined sitting in a parliament and taking policy decisions? Does discussing pressing issues give you an adrenaline rush? Does debating fuel you? If your answer to any of these is yes then congratulations and welcome to the BPD of Milan’20.

Judging Criteria

● Evidence - 13
● Comprehension - 10
● Effectiveness and persuasion skills 10
● Rebuttal - 12
● Delivery - 5 

Rules

● No. of participants per team - 2.
● Topic will be given on the spot.
● 15 minutes of preparation time will be given.
● Language permitted - English.
● Vulgarity and obscenity will not be tolerated.
● All participants must possess their college id cards.
● Judges decision is final and binding.
● Participant should reach the venue 60 minutes prior to the event

AD Pitch

In the era of digital marketing and advertising, fresh copywriting and content curation is a key skill. Think you’ve got it in you?

Rules

● Individual participation OR a team of 2 participants.
● Preparation time- 1 hour
● Presenting time - 5 Minutes
● Topic will be given on the spot.
● Components
- Taglines
- 50 word description
- Video Ad ideation
- Billboard Poster ideation
- Newspaper ad
- 2 Captions based on picture prompts of Service/object.
- 1 free to choose advertisement idea.
● Languages permitted - English
● Vulgarity and obscenity will not be tolerated.
● All participants must possess their college id cards.
● Judges decision is final and binding.
● Participant should reach the venue 60 minutes prior to the event 

Judging Criteria

● Appropriate evaluation of submitted material - 30
● Presentation (tone, body language, persuasive skills, creativity) - 20 

JOURNALISM

Journalists are nothing less than warriors of the truth, drafting history one article at a time. This Milan, try your hand at a reel life newsroom experience!

Rules

● Individual participation.
● Topic will be given on the spot.
● Rounds
- Write a 200-350 word article on the topic (time limit - 25 minutes)
- Present the news like a reporter (time limit 2-4 minutes)
- The third round is an impromptu round judging critical analysis and questioning skills, at the discretion of the judges.
● Languages permitted - English
● Vulgarity and obscenity will not be tolerated.
● All participants must possess their college id cards.
● Judges decision is final and binding.
● Participant should reach the venue 60 minutes prior to the event 

Judging Criteria

● Article - 20 points
● Reporting - 15 points
● Impromptu round - 15 points
