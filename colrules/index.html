general rules

  1. NOTE: No teams from SRM group of institutions will be allowed to participate in MILAN COLOSSEUM.
  2. REGISTRATION FEE: 400/- per member of the team including participants. No on spot registration will be entertained.
  3.   Accommodation is included in the registration amount.
  4. The last date for registration is 30th January 2020.
  5. Every participant must carry college id cards along with a valid government Id proof and 2 passport size photographs.
  6. The teams must bring an original copy of letter of authorization (N.O.C.), approved by the dean or director of the institute mentioning the names of the participants.
  7.  The type of tournament will be league/pool/knockout depending upon the number of teams registered.
  8.  Teams are requested to report to the respective venues 1 hour prior to the event. Maximum delay in reporting will be 15 minutes, else walkover will be given.
  9.  Fixtures shall not be changed for late arriving teams. No request pertaining to the same shall be entertained.
  10.   The decision of the referees/umpire/judges/officials will be final and binding.
  11.  Id cards issued at the time of registration should be carried at all times and must be produced on demand, if not produced the participant will be disqualified from the event.
  12. The team captain is responsible to take care of the discipline and decorum of his/her team.
  13. Acts of hooliganism, abusing, fighting, eve teasing, disrupting rules and harmony of the campus, vandalizing infrastructure or instruments are strictly against the rules of the institution.
  14. Boys are strictly prohibited from girlâ€™s place of accommodation at any time and vice-versa.
  15. Usage/consumption and/or possession of alcohol, drugs, controlled substances and devices of smoking, explosive substances, weapons are strictly prohibited.
  16. All the participants must obey the rules and regulation of SRM IST.
  17. Any in-disciplinary act would be severely penalized by the Milan Colosseum committee and the decision taken by the security committee of SRM IST will be final.
  18. Any game related issues should be reported to the Milan Colosseum committee within an hour of the incident, so that the organizing team members can act promptly and resolve the issues.
  19. No responsibility will be taken by the Milan Colosseum committee for unexpected bad weather conditions.

travel and accomodation

  1. Accommodation is provided for the participants at hostels of SRM IST from 12th February 2020 6:00 A.M. to 17th February 2020 12:00 P.M.
  2. Teams requiring accommodation are asked to fill the same in the registration form.
  3. Teams are requested to provide complete travel details which must include time and mode of arrival and departure on/before 8th February 2020.
  4. Teams are requested to contact the accommodation team atleast 24 hours before your arrival.
  5. The number of participants as a team/college/institution/university is advised to be updated with the accommodation team.
  6. It is requested for the teams to arrive together for smoother and faster registration.

inaugration ceremony

  1. The inauguration of MILAN Colosseum will commence at 3p.m. on 12th February 2020.
  2. Every team must be present for the inauguration ceremony along with their college flags and it is mandatory. Every team is required to bring their own university/institute/college flag ( 2 ft. * 3 ft. ).
