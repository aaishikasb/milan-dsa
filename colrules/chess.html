chess

Tournament type:

- Chess competition will be played in accordance with the “FIDE Laws of Chess”. The FIDE     Tournament Rules will be used in conjunction with the Laws of Chess.

- It will be an individual event.

- There will be no separate tournament for girls, but only a single tournament open to all.

- Depending upon number of entries, first round may be qualifier/ knockout.

time control:

Each player will get 90 minutes plus 30 seconds increment for every move from move number one.

All decisions given by Chief Arbiter will be final and are unquestionable.

IMPORTANT NOTICE:

RULES & SCHEDULE ARE SUBJECT TO CHANGE IN THE SPIRIT OF THE GAME .THE FINAL DECISION IN ALL MATTERS RESTS WITH THE MILAN COLOSSEUM TEAM. THE MANAGEMENT ALSO RESERVES THE RIGHT TO CANCEL AN EVENT AT ANY POINT OF THE FEST.
