table tennis

general rules:

- There can be a maximum of 4 players and a minimum of 3 players in each team.

- Matches will be held on the basis of two singles and one doubles.

- The order of play shall be as follows: 
    A vs. X 
    Doubles match
    B vs. Y

- A, B and X, Y shall be determined by order in which the names are given . The result of the     match shall be declared when either of the team wins 2 matches.

- Each game will be a ‘best of 5 match’ of 11 points.

- You are allowed to towel off every 6 points during a match, starting from 0-0.

- You are allowed a maximum rest period of 1 minute between games.

- Both the teams are required to report 1 hour before their matches. In case team does not report     latest after 20 minutes of the match time or is a player short due to any reason, it will be     disqualified and the other team will be declared as the winner.

- The decision of the referees will be final and all players must abide by it. 

IMPORTANT NOTICE:

RULES & SCHEDULE ARE SUBJECT TO CHANGE IN THE SPIRIT OF THE GAME .THE FINAL DECISION IN ALL MATTERS RESTS WITH THE MILAN COLOSSEUM TEAM. THE MANAGEMENT ALSO RESERVES THE RIGHT TO CANCEL AN EVENT AT ANY POINT OF THE FEST.
