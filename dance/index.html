Dance

They find beats in the monotony of life and aren’t afraid to dance to them. They spend hours of gruelling practice to perfect their moves on stage. Dance, an art form, has since millennia past, developed into a plethora of styles, with everyone putting their own spin to it.
Dance, is not just and art form. It’s a form of expression, of abstraction too. Words that cannot be conveyed by the moving of lips, are conveyed by the abhinaya. The feeling stuck in our hearts, in our minds, struggling to come out, show themselves in the expressions we out forth. A powerful too, and an even more powerful weapon, body language, has since evolved into dance, conveying to us, words that shall forever remain unsaid.
