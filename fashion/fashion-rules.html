Each team can have a maximum of 18 participants and a minimum of 9 participants. Team will be awarded negative marking for exceeding time limit.

Rules

• Each member of the team should strictly follow the theme.
• Time limit is a total of 15 min for each team ( 12 min stage time + 1     min designer Round + 2 min stage setup time.)
• Use of accessories such as fire,animals,explosives and color must     be avoided.
• No form of vulgarity would be tolerated.
• Music tracks should be submitted 2 hours prior to the show.
• Possession of college ID card is a must by every member of the     team.
• The participants must report at the venue least 60 minutes prior to     their event.

Judging Criteria

1. Walk
2. Apparel
3. Co-ordination
4. Sequence
5. Costume
6. Theme 

Mr. And Ms. MILAN Fashion

The participating team shall be nominating one member each for this event. 

ROUND 1

• Participants have to ramp walk on the     stage for 30 seconds to show their     personality and stage presence
• Those who qualify Round 1 show up for     Round 2 
• The participants must report at the venue     least 60 minutes prior to their event.

ROUND 2

• Participants would have to model for a     product for 30 seconds. They can give as     many poses as they want in that     duration
• The product for which they would be     modeling would be decided by picking     chits, just before they enter the stage
• They would be judged on the ability to     pose with the product and their     presence of mind
• Those who qualify Round 2 show up for     Round 3 
• The participants must report at the venue     least 60 minutes prior to their event.

ROUND 3

• This is a talent round where the     participants have to showcase their     talent on the stage for 1 min
• This will be followed by interactive     questioning round where the participant     will have to answer the question asked     by the judges
• The participants should bring their own     audio track to be played. Other specific     requirements for this round like musical     instruments etc. should be brought by     the participant
• Decision of the judges would be final.  
• The participants must report at the venue     least 60 minutes prior to their event.    

important 

• Every member of the team should possess the college ID card at the time of registration before every event
• The colleges must report 2 hours prior to the show for slot picking
• Sound tracks should be submitted 2hrs before the show.
• Green room available behind the stage
