VIBRATO

Western Solo
A competition based on the western vocal skills of a singer. It will focus on the different skills of a singer to render a song.

Details

TEAM SIZE: 1 Participant
TIME LIMIT: 3 minutes

JUDGING CRITERIA: Range, Tempo, Clarity

Rules

- All performances must be in English. Usage of any other languages can lead to disqualification.
- Time limit for each performance (including set up is 3 min).
- Each participant MUST have a college ID for verification during Milan registration.
- A single accompanist or a karaoke (backing track without vocal) will be allowed.
- The decision of the judges will be final.
- The participants should report at the venue 60 minutes prior to the event.

ALANKAR

Eastern Solo

TIME LIMIT: 3 minutes

JUDGING CRITERIA: Range, Tempo, Clarity

- Each participant MUST have a college id for verification during Milan registration.
- A single accompanist or a karaoke (backing track without vocal) will be allowed.
- The decision of the judges will be final.
- The participants should report at the venue 60 minutes prior to the event.

ACAPELLA

A competition to showcase complete talent and creativity without the use of instruments.

Details

TEAM SIZE: 3-8 Members

TIME LIMIT: 7 minutes

Judging Criteria

Accuracy, Tone Quality, Tempo, Dynamic, Energy, Transitions.

Rules

- No backing tracks or instruments are allowed.
- Team may have singers/beat boxers/rappers etc. in any number within the team size limit.
- Time limit including set up is 7 mins.
- Each participant MUST have a college id for verification during Milan registration.
- The decision of the judges will be final. 
- The participants should report at the venue 60 minutes prior to the event.

RESONANCE

BOB Western
A contest in which bands of western genre of music compete to showcase their best set.

Details

TEAM SIZE: Minimun 4 - Maximum 10 Participants
TIME LIMIT: 15 minutes

JUDGING CRITERIA: Tempo, Tightness, Clarity, Improvisation and Execution, Stage Presence, Synchronisation.

Rules

- This is a western band competition for college bands only.
- Use of any language apart from English will lead to disqualification.
- Every participant MUST have a college id for verification during Milan registration.
- Backing tracks or loopers are not permitted.
- Original compositions will be given more weightage. However, the final decision regarding the same will be taken by the judges.
- Time limit including set up is 15 mins.
- Participants are requested to get their own instruments. A standard drum kit will be provided at the venue. 
- The participants should report at the venue 60 minutes prior to the event.

BEAT DROP

Beat Boxing
An event to assemble to showcase and compete with the best beat boxers in town.

Details

TEAM SIZE: 1 Participant
TIME LIMIT: 3 minutes

JUDGING CRITERIA: Harmony and balance, Clarity, Musicality, Complexity, Originality

Rules

- No instruments are permitted.
- One accompanist is allowed.
- No recorded tracks.
- Competitors should not perform beat box routines as already performed in public by other beat boxers, to the best of their knowledge. Intellectual property rights must be respected.
- Judge’s decision is final. In terms of a tiebreaker, an additional round will be given for the competitors.
- Do not bite (copy) someone else’s work. New/fresh stuff is more than welcome. 
- The participants should report at the venue 60 minutes prior to the event.

SPINBACK

DJ Battle
An event to showcase DJ-ing skills and techniques to enthuse the audience.

Details

TEAM SIZE: 1 PARTICIPANT
TIME LIMIT: 7 MINS (5+2 MIN SETUP) 

Judging Criteria

Creativity, Track Selection, Techniques and crowd response.

Rules

- A time limit of 5 min fixed per set, although there is no limit on the number of tracks that can be used.
- After the set, judges will score the participants on a scale of 1-10.
- Top two highest scores will compete in the final round.
- The top two DJs will have 10 min to impress the judges and the crowd.
- Final results will be decided based on the judges’ scoring and the crowd’s response.
- The participants should report at the venue 60 minutes prior to the event. 

DAW MASTERS

Music Production
An event to showcase creativity in music arrangements using individual skills, techniques and style.

Details

TEAM SIZE- 1 PARTICIPANT
TIME LIMIT – 60 MINS 

Judging Criteria

Judging will be based on several criteria which include creativity, skills, methods used etc.

Rules

- No samples other than the those given on the spot would be allowed to be used.
- Judges would be constantly monitoring the sessions in order to prevent any malpractice.
- Laptops and other required equipment are to be brought by each participant.
- Participants are allowed to use any music production software(DAW) that they are comfortable with.
- The participants should report at the venue 60 minutes prior to the event.
